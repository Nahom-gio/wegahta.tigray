Wegahta

Wegahta, a charitable civil society organization established with the noble intention of making a meaningful difference in the lives of those affected by the scourge of war and drought.

Our work

Wegahta's priorities include both short-term humanitarian interventions and long-term sustainable solutions. In the short term, we focus on alleviating the immediate suffering caused by conflict and drought, tackling issues such as famine, lack of access to education, insufficient medical supplies, and financial hardship. .
