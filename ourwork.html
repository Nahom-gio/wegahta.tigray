About Us

Wegahta is a registered non-governmental organisation established by Tigrayans born in Addis Ababa. We obtained registration and legal recognition under the Ethiopian Civil Society Organisation Proclamation Number 1113/2019 and registration number 5589, issued on May 10, 2021.

Our Commitment to Research

Wegahta boasts a cadre of skilled and educated individuals eager to lend their expertise to the research efforts focused on Tigray's challenges. Our team stands ready to delve into key areas including agriculture, education, healthcare, tourism, and the sustainable utilization of available resources.
By leveraging our collective knowledge and resources, we aim to generate actionable insights that can effectively combat the challenges facing the region. Join us in our mission to drive positive change and create a brighter future for all residents of Tigray.

Awareness of Tigray's Challenges and Opportunities

Presently, Tigray grapples with a multitude of pressing issues, ranging from drought-induced famine and widespread displacement to critical shortages in medical supplies, educational resources, and nutritional support for children. Additionally, access to clean water for both consumption and irrigation remains a significant concern, compounded by erratic rainfall patterns and high rates of unemployment.

Brand Partnerships

Beyond The Well, a select group of companies and foundations have chosen to support our staff salaries and operations through corporate gifts, percentage of sales, and campaigns tailored to each business' market and brand.

Investment Earnings

We utilise industry best practices in managing investment assets to bring clean water to the most people. Operating funds and committed donor funds awaiting deployment are invested safely in capital markets with a goal to grow assets. This multiplies the impact charity: water and its donors have on the water crisis over time.
